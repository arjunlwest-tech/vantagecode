🔒 Privacy Policy

Your Data.

1. Introduction

Vantage Coding, a product of Vantage Suites, respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our gamified coding platform.

2. Data We Collect

  • Local Storage: Your XP, quest progress, notes, and preferences are stored locally in your browser. We do not send this data to any server.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous, aggregated data to improve the platform experience.
  • Cookies: Used by third-party analytics and advertising services.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized ads via Google Ads Settings.

4. Data Retention

All user data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. You can clear your data at any time through your browser settings. We do not maintain server-side copies of your personal data.

Questions about this policy? Reach us at privacy@vantagesuites.com
